Your Financial Journey

Why planning today changes everything

Financial planning isn't about restricting yourself. It's about designing freedom — so that one day, money is a choice, not a constraint.

AGE 25–30
Build the Foundation
Emergency fund (6 months expenses). Term life insurance. Health insurance. Start SIP — even ₹3,000/month. At this stage, time is your unfair advantage. Don't waste it.
AGE 30–40
Accelerate Growth
Increase SIP with every salary hike. Review your portfolio annually. Add ELSS for tax efficiency. Start goal-based investing: child's education, home down-payment. Eliminate high-interest debt.
AGE 40–50
Consolidate & Protect
Shift 20–30% to safer instruments as retirement approaches. Maximise NPS contributions. Estate planning. Review insurance cover — your liabilities and dependents have grown.
AGE 50–60
Transition to Freedom
Gradually de-risk portfolio. Build a 2-year cash buffer. Plan SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) strategy. Healthcare insurance top-up. Congratulations — you planned. Most people don't.
